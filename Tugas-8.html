General

Kaedehara Kazuha is a playable Anemo character in Genshin Impact.

A wandering samurai of the once-famed Kaedehara Clan with an ability to read the sounds of nature, Kazuha is a temporary crewmember of The Crux. Despite being burdened by the many happenings of his past, he still maintains an easygoing disposition.

Gameplay

Vision: Anemo  |  Weapon: Sword  |  Region: Inazuma

Kazuha excels as a support character, providing crowd control and elemental damage buffs through his Elemental Mastery scaling.

Constellations

C1 – Scarlet Hills: Decreases Chihayaburu's CD by 10%. Using Kazuha Slash resets the CD of Chihayaburu.

C2 – Yamaarashi Tailwind: The Autumn Whirlwind field increases Elemental Mastery of Kazuha and all party members by 200.
